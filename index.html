Our Story

Our family loves good food — that's part of being raised on a farm in Idaho. We're always tinkering in the kitchen, looking for new tastes and exploring interesting ideas.

After a butter-making project, we found ourselves with a couple gallons of fresh buttermilk. No one can eat that many buttermilk biscuits, so we spent the next few days experimenting with different ingredients. We were amazed at how much we loved the drinks.

Buttermilk is a superfood — packed with protein, probiotic cultures, electrolytes, and a beautiful light, tangy flavor that combines beautifully with savory and sweet ingredients.

Not your grandma's buttermilk.
